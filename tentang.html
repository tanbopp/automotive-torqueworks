About TorqueWorks

Our Journey in Serving Indonesia's Automotive Needs

Company History

TorqueWorks Indonesia was founded in 2014 by a group of experienced automotive technicians who had the vision to provide the best service in the field of vehicle spare parts and services.

Starting from a small workshop in South Jakarta, we have grown to become one of the trusted automotive service providers in Indonesia. With our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we continue to innovate in providing the best solutions.

Now, TorqueWorks has served thousands of customers throughout Indonesia with various services ranging from spare parts sales, vehicle service, to unique and quality custom modifications.
